Core Team Recuirtment

Developer Student Club is a program powered by Google Developers that identifies and supports university students who are passionate about growing developer communities that foster learning, sharing, and collaboration. The club is open to any student, ranging from novice developers, who are just starting, to advanced developers, who want to enhance their skill set. The club is intended as a space for students to collaborate and try out new ideas for existing problems using mobile and web technologies.

Privacy

1) All the data collected during the recuirtment process are secured and no data is breached out for any reason.
2) All the data collected from the user is stored securely in Google Firebase Cloud.
3) Personal Information like mobile number, email address and user name are collected as per recuirtment process requirement.
4) Your data related to your skills and personality will not be shared for any reason.
5) Every data insertion to the cloud are done only with authentication.

Aware Of

1) Your entry to the recuirtment form is done with a guest code of 8 digit number provided by your respective DSC team.
2) Guest code helps you to get into the form filling area.
3) Your are anonymously authenticated once and your given a unique user id from server.
4) All the data you enter get stored with reference to your user id.
5) For any queries or updation about your data please do use your user id for reference.
6) A user can apply only once so do it carefully.
7) Note that if you try to breach or do something inappropriate your guest id will be blocked and you cannot apply any more.
